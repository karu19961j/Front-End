ABOUT US

Over the past few decades, Indian charcoal sector has witnessed remarkable growth and development. Moreover, the number of charcoal manufacturers across the nation is increasing day by day. We, Sagar Charcoal Depot, are a name to reckon with quality. Established in the year 1980, we are successfully catering to the requirements of our large customer base through a qualitative range of Charcoal and allied products. We are well known as one of the leading manufacturers, traders, suppliers and distributors of the industry. We are offering a wide gamut of Activated Charcoal, Hard Wood Charcoal, Coconut Charcoal, Coal, Hard Coke, Charcoal Dust, and more. We lay strong emphasis on maintaining high quality standards in our products as well as processes. Our entire array of products is developed under the supervision of highly qualified professionals who have years of experience in this field. In order to complete clients’ order on time, we have developed a robust manufacturing facility which is installed with latest technology machinery and equipment. With our persistent efforts & commitment, we have been able to develop amicable relation with our large number of customers.

Our Infrastructure

We are empowered with a strong infrastructural facility that allows us to ensure bulk production of our products in the minimum lead time. We have set up a research and development department which is backed by the expertise of experienced research analysts. Consistently engaged in conducting thorough market research, our research personnel help us in improvisation of our products as well as processes. In addition, we have developed an advanced manufacturing facility which is outfitted with modern set of machinery as well as latest technology. All the machines are regularly upgraded in order to keep pace with the changing market demands along with maintaining higher work standards.

Quality Control

Quality is given pivotal importance by us. Ever since the inception, we are committed to fulfill our clients’ requirements though a quality assured range of Powdered Charcoal, Natural Wood Charcoal, Hard Coke, etc. We make use of finest grade raw material such as wood for the manufacturing purpose. The wood is processed under the expert team of quality controllers who possess rich industry experience. Beginning from the raw material procurement till the final dispatch, they monitor each and every stage in order to ascertain optimum grade end products. Moreover, our quality analysts also ensure that all the processes are carried out in compliance with the set industry standards.
