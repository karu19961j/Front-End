About-us

One Stand United LLC. is a logistics consulting, brokerage, and private equity firm that has one of the highest track records of growth within the US. We specialize in operating, managing, and acquiring companies in several industries including the freight and trucking industry, the technology industry, the transportation industry, the warehousing industry, and the food industry. Our motto is high standards of success, and we always put the needs, concerns, and interests of our clients first. As a small team of experts in the industries we choose to invest in, we are dedicated to building up the portfolios of promising companies whether through direct investment or offering our consulting services.

Logistic Consulting
Trucking and transportation is one of the most overlooked areas for operational savings, yet it provides one of the fastest Return on Investment (ROI). More than mere trickle down economics, the trucking and transportations savings can significantly impact profitability and shareholder value. The companies leveraging One Stand United’s logistics consulting and operational management services for their daily transportation operations are saving 5-20% on their annual costs. One Stand United’s Consult-Implement-Operate (CIO) business model enables us to provide a wide range of transportation services for all types of shippers (distributors, retailers and manufacturers) with multiple carriers and modes of transportation. Our experienced operational team can serve as your transportation team or augment your existing expertise and capabilities.
