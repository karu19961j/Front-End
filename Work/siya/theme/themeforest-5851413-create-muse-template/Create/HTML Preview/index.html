OUR STORY

Our Inception was in the Year 1989 at our First Plant at Kachigam, Daman by our Founder Shri. Sanjeev Agarwal. A Textile Engineer by Education and Profession he was always aware of the ever changing needs of Packaging of the Customer. He had started out with the Manufacturing of Corrugated Boxes mainly for the POY & FDY Yarn manufacturers. In the Year 1998 after seeing the demand of Textile Tubes with his Customers Mr. Sanjeev Agarwal started with the Paper Tube division at Siyaram Packaging. He started out with the manufacturing of Textile Tubes for POY & FDY Yarn. We are one of the Pioneers in the industry to start out with the manufacturing of Textiles Tubes. After starting out with POY & FDY Paper Tubes we have further expanded into various other kinds of speciality cores such as Polyfim Cores, Paper Mill Cores, BOPP Tape cores, Composite Containers and many more……

OUR MENTOR

Shri Sanjeev Agarwal has a wealth of Experience in the Paper & Packaging Industry. All those who know him well would agree that he has done a ‘PhD’ in the field of packaging through his sheer experience. A textile engineer by Education he completed his B.Tech in Textile Engineering in the year 1979. Since then he has been closely associated with the Textile Industry and is well aware about the requirements of the Industry. This helps us greatly to judge the exact needs of our Customers and help us Tailor Make our Product for our customer.

OUR VISION

To be a Brand Name synonymous for Quality and Consistency in the market. >Welcome to SIYARAM PACKAGING PVT LTD< Siyaram Packaging Pvt Ltd is one of the Leading manufacturer and Exporter of Textile & Non Textile Paper Tubes, Paper Containers, Corrugated Boxes, Core Boards as well as New & Used Machinery for Packaging.

PRODUCT and SERVICES

  •  

    Textile Tubes

    Spiral Paper Tubes for Yarns such as Partially Oriented Yarn (POY), Fully Drawn Yarn ( FDY), Nylon, Highly Oriented Yarn ( HOY), Tyre Cord Yarn, Draw Texturised Yarn ( DTY) are made by us.

  •  

    Non Textile Paper Cores

    Cores which are used in the Polyfilms industries need high dimensional stability so that the wrapping of the Poyfilm is uniform. We provide Wax Coated as well as Resin Coated Cores for the Polyfilm industry which provide dimensional stability as well as high Compression Strength.

  •  

    Core Board Kraft Paper

    We have recently started with the Export of Core Board specialized for Paper Tubes. The Core Board is Slitted into smaller coils as per the Customers requirement and shrink- wrapped to protect the Paper from atmospheric changes. This helps the Customers to save greatly in the Cost of Slitting as well as the wastage of the entire production process.

  •  

    Corrugated Boxe

    In order to provide a Cost Effective and Technically sound way of Packaging to our Customers we have designed High CS boxes in 3 Layer instead of 5 Layer or 7 Layer. Using our Machinery Prowess we have successfully designed and replaced 5 Layer Corrugated Boxes with 3 Layer Boxes. This provides us customer with a cost effective and high quality product for their packaging needs.
